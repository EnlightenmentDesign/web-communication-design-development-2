Form & form elements

Form

From a markup standpoint I would recommend encapsulating your form label and field in a div as seen below. This will provide more flexibility to what you can do when styling and structuring.

Layout & structure

Table 3.1 - Form structure
Markup Example 
<form
    method="post"
    action="registration.php"
    name="registration"
>
    Form controls, labels, fieldsets
</form>
 
<fieldset>
    <legend>...</legend>
</fieldset>
Demographics 
<div class="form-group row">
    <label for="firstName" class="col-sm-2 col-form-label">First Name:</label>
    <div class="col-sm-10">
        <input type="text" name="firstName" id="firstName" class="form-control" />
    </div>
</div>
 
<div class="form-check">
    <input class="form-check-input" type="radio" name="radio" id="radio" value="option1" checked>
    <label class="form-check-label" for="radio">
        Default radio
    </label>
</div>
